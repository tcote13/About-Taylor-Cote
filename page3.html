What I Do!

Throughout my life, I have been driven by a desire to constantly challenge myself and grow, which has led me to have a wide range of work experiences. From the food industry to home childcare, I have always been eager to learn new skills and take on new challenges. Currently, I am working as a tour guide at Elon University, where I have the opportunity to meet new people and show them around the campus that I love so much. During the summers, I work as a head counselor at a summer camp for 3-5 year olds, which not only allows me to spend time by the beach in my hometown of Rhode Island but also gives me the joy of working with children. In 2021, I completed an internship at a marketing and advertising firm, which was a valuable experience that helped me shape my academic and professional goals. It was through this internship that I realized my passion for Strategic Communications and Media Analytics, which ultimately led me to pursue my degree at Elon.Aside from work and academics, I have a love for playing field hockey and photography. I am actively seeking more involvement on campus and enjoy attending cultural events. I also hold leadership roles as a Senator and Chief of Staff on the student government and will be running for Student Body President next year. As I look towards the future, I am excited to see where my experiences will take me. Ideally, I hope to live near the beach, where I can continue to pursue my passions and find new opportunities to grow. I am excited for all the possibilities that lie ahead and am ready to seize every opportunity that comes my way.
