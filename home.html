About Me!

Born and raised in the beautiful state of Rhode Island, I am a passionate lover of the Ocean State and its breathtaking coastlines. Growing up, I attended a prestigious country day school from kindergarten through to high school graduation in 2021. I come from a loving family, with my mom, dad, and younger sister, along with my furry companion Finn. During my time in high school, I was an active athlete, playing field hockey and basketball. But my true love was found in the water, where I spent countless hours swimming, surfing, and basking in the sun at the beach. Aside from sports, I developed a passion for photography, capturing the beauty of nature and candid moments. When I'm not behind the camera or in the water, you can find me hiking, exploring new places, and practicing field hockey.I have now ventured out of my comfort zone, currently pursuing a degree at a university in a different state. I am excited to embark on new adventures, meet new people, and learn from the diverse experiences that await me. Despite being away from home, I carry the love and memories of Rhode Island with me wherever I go.
