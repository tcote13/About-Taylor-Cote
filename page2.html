Moving from RI to NC

As a proud student at Elon University in North Carolina, I am thrilled to be living out my dreams at my first choice school. I am currently studying Strategic Communications and Media Analytics, with a minor in Psychology. The Communications Department at Elon has proven to be an impressive program, providing me with a wealth of knowledge and experiences that have helped me grow both personally and academically.While I have grown to love the beauty of North Carolina, I can't help but feel a slight longing for the coastal charm of my hometown. Nevertheless, I am grateful for the new experiences and perspectives I have gained while being a part of the Elon community. As I navigate through my academic journey, I am excited to see where it will take me in the future. Although I am not sure where I will end up after graduation, one thing is certain: I do not see myself returning to the north. I am eager to explore the various opportunities that will arise and create my own path, one that aligns with my passions and ambitions.
